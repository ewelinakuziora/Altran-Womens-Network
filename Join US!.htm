WE WANT YOU TO BE PART OF IT!

 

Altran team would like to invite ALL of You High-Flying women to the official launch of the Altran Women’s Network in Switzerland, to be held at the Sheraton Hotel on the 26th of September.

 

Our Inspiring Women evening will be a chance for you to network with other like-minded women and listen to our Panel Discussion led by Patricia Widmer, Program Head “Women Back to Business”, University of St. Gallen. Our dynamic panel of female leaders will be discussing their diverse career paths, shared gender struggles, work-life balance and how women can easily empower each other both professionally and personally.

 

Panel Executive Guests:

·      Birgit Wemhoff – Global Sales Leader at General Electric Switzerland GmbH

·      Eva Schönleitner – Group Vice President Digital Partnerships ABB Management Services Ltd

·      Florence Helg – Team Manager at Altran Switzerland AG
